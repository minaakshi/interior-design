Government Polytechnic(GP),Saharsa is a government polytechnic college in Saharsa district of Bihar. The institution has been continuously playing an important role in shaping the career of young students of Bihar. The Institution has produced distinguished Engineers and Technocrats in the past. In the past 56 years, GP Saharsa has earned fame and recognition due to the success of our Alumni.
